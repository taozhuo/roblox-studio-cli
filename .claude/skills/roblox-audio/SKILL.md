# Audio Reference

## Quick Reference

- **Audio assets**: Explore how to find and import audio assets for use in your experiences.
- **Audio effects**: Audio effects modify or enhance audio streams in your experiences.
- **Audio**: An overview of audio features on Roblox.
- **Audio objects**: An overview of modular audio objects on Roblox.
- **Dynamic effects**: Legacy effects that modify or enhance Sound objects in your experiences.
- **Sound groups**: Legacy audio mixers that group multiple Sound objects so you can control the properties of multiple audio signals at once.
- **Sound**: An overview of legacy Sound objects and effects.
- **Sound objects**: Sound objects are legacy instances that emit sound within an experience.
